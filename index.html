FC Pro World Championship

The FC Pro World Championship is a premier FIFAe event, taking place in Berlin, Germany. It features 32 elite EA SPORTS FC players competing for the prestigious title and a share of the $1 million prize pool. This thrilling tournament showcases the pinnacle of virtual football talent and strategy, promising an electrifying atmosphere and intense matches that will captivate fans worldwide.

VALORANT Champions Tour 2025

The VALORANT Champions Tour (VCT) 2025 is Riot Games' fifth official tournament circuit. It features top VALORANT teams from around the globe, competing in various stages such as regional Kickoff events, Masters tournaments, and the grand finale, Champions, held in Paris. The competition spans multiple regions, including the Americas, China, EMEA, and Pacific, each hosting its own events.
